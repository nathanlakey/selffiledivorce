# South Dakota Divorce Without Children (2026)

With no minor children, South Dakota divorce is among the simplest and fastest in the country — $95 fee, no residency minimum, no waiting period, Separation Agreement filed with the Complaint.

## The Agreed Divorce Process (No Children)

1. Confirm South Dakota domicile (no minimum length)
2. Inventory all marital and separate property
3. Draft and finalize the **Separation Agreement**; both sign and notarize
4. Download forms at ujs.sd.gov/self_help/family.aspx
5. File Complaint **and Separation Agreement** at Circuit Court in your county; pay $95
6. Serve Respondent — or obtain Acceptance of Service
7. No waiting period — request hearing date immediately
8. Attend hearing; judge reviews Separation Agreement; Decree of Divorce entered

---

## Separation Agreement — What to Cover (No Children)

### Marital Real Property
- Legal description; agreed FMV; mortgage balance; marital equity
- Assignment: one keeps (buyout; refinancing deadline; fallback; Quitclaim Deed → SD Register of Deeds) or sale (proceeds split; timeline)

### Marital Financial Accounts
- Institution, type, balance; assignment; transfer

### Retirement Accounts
- QDRO for employer plans
- IRA: transfer incident to divorce
- SDRS (South Dakota Retirement System): sdrs.sd.gov — DRO after Decree

### Vehicles
- Assignment; loan assumption; SD DMV title transfer

### Marital Debts
- Creditor, balance, who assumes, indemnification

### Alimony
Award — or explicit waiver:
"Each party waives any and all claims for alimony, now and forever."

### Separate Property Acknowledgment
State each spouse's separate property; confirm it remains with that spouse.

---

## Post-Divorce Steps (No Children)

- Record Quitclaim Deed at South Dakota Register of Deeds in property's county
- QDRO for employer retirement plans
- SDRS DRO for state employees — sdrs.sd.gov
- Update vehicle titles — South Dakota DMV
- Restore name: SD DMV → Social Security → accounts
- Update all beneficiary designations
