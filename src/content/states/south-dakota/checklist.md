# South Dakota Divorce Checklist — Step-by-Step (2026)

---

## Phase 1 — Pre-Filing Assessment

- [ ] Confirm South Dakota domicile (no minimum length required)
- [ ] Confirm grounds: "Irreconcilable differences"
- [ ] Gather all financial documents: income, bank accounts, investments, real estate, retirement, debts
- [ ] Identify Circuit Court location in your county
- [ ] Download forms: ujs.sd.gov/self_help/family.aspx

---

## Phase 2 — Financial Inventory

- [ ] All real estate: fair market value, mortgage balance, marital vs. separate
- [ ] Bank and investment accounts: balances, marital vs. separate
- [ ] Retirement accounts: total balance; marital portion (contributions during marriage)
- [ ] Vehicles: values and loans
- [ ] All debts: creditor, balance, marital vs. separate
- [ ] Business interests (if any)
- [ ] Monthly income documentation

---

## Phase 3 — Draft and Execute the Separation Agreement

South Dakota files the Separation Agreement with the Complaint — complete this before filing.

- [ ] All marital real property addressed (assignment, buyout, sale; Quitclaim Deed → SD Register of Deeds)
- [ ] All marital financial accounts assigned
- [ ] Retirement accounts: QDRO for employer plans; IRA transfer language
- [ ] Vehicles assigned; South Dakota DMV title transfer
- [ ] All marital debts assigned; indemnification language
- [ ] Separate property acknowledged and confirmed
- [ ] **Alimony:** award with terms — or explicit waiver
- [ ] If children: legal custody, physical custody, Parenting Plan, child support per SD Guidelines
- [ ] Both spouses sign and notarize the Separation Agreement

---

## Phase 4 — Complete Court Forms

Forms: ujs.sd.gov/self_help/family.aspx

- [ ] **Complaint for Divorce** (Circuit Court form)
- [ ] **Summons**
- [ ] Acceptance of Service (if spouse cooperates)
- [ ] **Separation Agreement** (filed with or shortly after Complaint)
- [ ] Proposed **Decree of Divorce**
- [ ] If children: **Parenting Plan**; **Child Support Worksheet** per SD Guidelines

---

## Phase 5 — File at Circuit Court

- [ ] File Complaint and Separation Agreement at the Circuit Court in your county
- [ ] Pay **$95** filing fee
- [ ] Receive case number
- [ ] Serve Respondent (or obtain Acceptance of Service)
- [ ] File Proof of Service / Acceptance of Service

---

## Phase 6 — No Waiting Period

- [ ] No mandatory waiting period in South Dakota — proceed immediately
- [ ] Request a hearing date from the Circuit Court clerk

---

## Phase 7 — Final Hearing / Decree

- [ ] Appear at hearing
- [ ] Judge reviews Separation Agreement and Parenting Plan (if applicable)
- [ ] Judge enters **Decree of Divorce**
- [ ] Obtain certified copies

---

## Phase 8 — Post-Divorce Steps

- [ ] Real estate: Quitclaim Deed → **South Dakota Register of Deeds** in county where property is located
- [ ] QDRO for employer retirement plans
- [ ] South Dakota state employees: South Dakota Retirement System (SDRS) — sdrs.sd.gov — DRO
- [ ] Vehicles: South Dakota DMV
- [ ] Name restoration: SD DMV → Social Security → accounts
- [ ] Beneficiary designations: life insurance, retirement, payable-on-death accounts
