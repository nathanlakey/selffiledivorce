# Utah Divorce Without Children (2026)

With no minor children, Utah divorce is more straightforward — no Divorce Orientation class required, simpler paperwork, and the MyPaperwork tool makes form completion fast and affordable.

---

| Factor | Rule |

## Step-by-Step (No Children)

### 1 — Confirm 90-Day County Residency
Either spouse must have lived in the filing county for at least 90 days.

### 2 — Use MyPaperwork ($20) — No Orientation Required
Go to utcourts.gov/en/self-help/divorce.html → MyPaperwork → Divorce → No Minor Children.
Complete the guided interview. Download all generated forms.

### 3 — Both Parties Complete Financial Declarations
Required in all Utah divorces. Comprehensive income, asset, and debt disclosure.

### 4 — Draft and Execute the Marital Settlement Agreement
Resolve all property, debts, and alimony. Both sign and notarize.

### 5 — File at Utah District Court; Pay $325

### 6 — Service (or Acceptance)
Respondent has 21 days to respond after personal service.

### 7 — 30-Day Waiting Period
Apply for waiver only with extraordinary circumstances and a formal motion.

### 8 — Final Hearing or Entry on Papers
Brief hearing or administrative entry — varies by county.

### 9 — Decree of Divorce Entered

## Marital Settlement Agreement — What to Cover (No Children)

### Marital Real Property
- Full legal description; agreed FMV; mortgage balance; marital equity
- One keeps: buyout + refinancing deadline (90–180 days) + Deed → **Utah County Recorder**
- Or sale: net proceeds split after payoff and closing costs

### Marital Financial Accounts
Institution, type, balance; assignment; transfer instructions

### Retirement Accounts
- QDRO for ERISA employer plans
- IRA: transfer incident to divorce — direct rollover
- Utah Retirement Systems (URS): urs.org — DRO procedures for state employees

### Vehicles
Assignment; loan assumption; Utah DMV title transfer

### Marital Debts
Creditor, account number, balance, who assumes, indemnification

### Alimony
Award with terms (standard of living primary factor per Utah Code § 30-3-5) — or explicit waiver:
"Each party waives any and all claims for alimony, now and forever."

### Separate Property Acknowledgment
List each spouse's separate property; confirm it remains with that spouse

## Post-Divorce Steps (No Children)

- Record Quitclaim Deed at **Utah County Recorder** in the correct county
- QDRO for employer retirement plans
- Utah Retirement Systems (URS) DRO: urs.org
- Update vehicle titles — Utah DMV
- Name restoration: Utah DMV → Social Security → bank accounts
- Update all beneficiary designations immediately (life insurance, 401k/IRA, POD accounts)
