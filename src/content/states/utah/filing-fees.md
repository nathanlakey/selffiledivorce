# Utah Divorce Filing Fees and Costs (2026)

---

## Court Filing Fee

| Fee | Amount |
|---|---|
| Petition for Divorce | **$325** |
| Certified copies of Decree | $4–$8 per copy |

---

## MyPaperwork — Utah's $20 Form System

| Item | Cost |
|---|---|
| MyPaperwork guided form generation (utcourts.gov) | **$20** |

This is the best value of any state's paid official form system. The $20 fee covers all forms for your specific situation — with children, without, with property, etc. Available at utcourts.gov/en/self-help/divorce.html.

## Fee Waiver — Financial Hardship

Utah waives court filing fees for qualifying low-income filers.

**How to apply:**
1. Complete the **Motion and Affidavit to Waive Fees** (available at utcourts.gov)
2. File with the Petition
3. Court approves; fee waived or deferred

**Utah Legal Services:** utahlegalservices.org — free civil legal help statewide.
**Utah State Bar Lawyer Referral:** utahbar.org

---

## Utah County Recorder — Deed Recording

After divorce, real estate deed changes are recorded at the **Utah County Recorder** in the county where the property is located.

Fee: approximately $30–$40 per document. Contact the specific county recorder for current fees.
