# Georgia Divorce Filing Fees by County (2026)

Georgia has some of the lowest divorce filing fees in the country — typically $215–$230 statewide. Here's a county-by-county breakdown and everything else you'll need to budget for.

## Georgia's Filing Fee Advantage

Georgia's filing fees are dramatically lower than many other states:
- Texas: $300–$400
- California: $435–$450
- Florida: $395–$410

This is a significant advantage for budget-conscious filers and a great content angle for the site.

## Can I Get the Fee Waived?

Yes. File an **Affidavit of Indigence** with the Clerk of Superior Court. If the court determines you cannot afford the fee, it will be waived.
