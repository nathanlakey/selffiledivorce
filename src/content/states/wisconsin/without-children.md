# Wisconsin Divorce Without Children (2026)

Wisconsin divorce without children is more streamlined but still subject to the 120-day mandatory waiting period. The community property framework means starting negotiations from a 50/50 position.

---

## Overview

| Factor | Rule |
|---|---|
| Minimum time | 120 days from service date |
| Filing fee | $184 (no children) |
| Property presumption | 50/50 equal split |
| Marital pot | Marital property only (individual property protected) |
| Maintenance | Discretionary; 10 statutory factors |
| Forms | wicourts.gov/formdisplay (FA-series) |
| Financial Disclosure | Required from both parties |

## What the Marital Settlement Agreement Must Cover (No Children)

### All Marital Real Property
- Identify as marital (acquired during marriage) vs. individual (pre-marital)
- For marital real estate: agreed value; 50/50 equity split (or documented deviation); who keeps or sale terms
- Refinancing deadline; fallback if not met
- Quit Claim Deed → Register of Deeds after refinancing

### Individual Real Property
- Confirm stays with original owner
- Include waiver of claim by other spouse
- If mortgage: confirm refinancing to remove the non-owner

### All Marital Financial Accounts
- Bank accounts opened or funded during marriage → marital → 50/50 split or assign
- Investment accounts → identify marital portion
- Retirement accounts → marital portion; QDRO for employer plans; IRA transfer for IRAs
- Specify the division formula: "50% of the 401k balance as of [dissolution date]" or "50% of marital contributions from [marriage date] to [judgment date]"

### Vehicles
- Vehicles purchased during marriage → marital → assign; refinance out of joint loan
- Pre-marital vehicles → individual → confirmed to original owner

### Marital Debts
- Each marital debt assigned to one spouse; indemnification

### Individual Debts
- Pre-marital debts remain with the original debtor; confirm in MSA

### Maintenance
Options:
1. Maintenance order: specify amount, duration, termination events
2. **Waiver:** "Each party waives any claim for maintenance, now and in the future."

Include one — do not leave maintenance unaddressed.

## Financial Disclosure Statements

Both parties must complete and exchange **Financial Disclosure Statements** (FA-4138V or FA-4139V). Required even with no children and a complete agreement. File both with the court.
