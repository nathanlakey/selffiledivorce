# Wyoming Divorce Checklist — Step-by-Step (2026)

---

## Phase 1 — Pre-Filing Assessment

- [ ] Confirm 60-day Wyoming residency in the filing county (either spouse)
- [ ] Confirm grounds: irreconcilable differences
- [ ] Determine process: joint/uncontested or individual/contested
- [ ] Gather all financial documents: income, bank accounts, investments, real estate, retirement, debts
- [ ] Identify Wyoming District Court in your county
- [ ] Download forms: courts.state.wy.us/court-self-help

---

## Phase 2 — Financial Inventory

- [ ] All real estate: FMV, mortgage balance, marital vs. separate
- [ ] Bank and investment accounts: balances, marital vs. separate
- [ ] Retirement accounts: total balance; marital portion
- [ ] Vehicles: values and loans
- [ ] All debts: creditor, balance, marital vs. separate
- [ ] Monthly income and expense documentation
- [ ] Business interests (if any)

---

## Phase 3 — Draft and Execute Separation Agreement (Before Filing)

Wyoming practice: complete the Separation Agreement **before filing** — it is filed with the Complaint.

- [ ] All marital real property addressed — assign, buyout, or sale
  - FMV; mortgage balance; marital equity
  - One keeps: buyout + refinancing deadline + Deed → **Wyoming County Clerk**
  - Or sale: proceeds split
- [ ] All marital financial accounts assigned
- [ ] Retirement accounts: QDRO for employer plans; IRA transfer; Wyoming WRS DRO
- [ ] Vehicles assigned; Wyoming DMV transfer
- [ ] All marital debts assigned; indemnification language
- [ ] Separate property confirmed
- [ ] **Alimony:** award (short-term more typical; long-term rarely awarded in Wyoming) — or explicit waiver
- [ ] If children: legal custody, physical custody, Parenting Plan, child support per Wyoming Guidelines
- [ ] Both spouses sign and notarize Separation Agreement

---

## Phase 4 — Complete Court Forms

Forms: courts.state.wy.us/court-self-help

- [ ] **Complaint for Divorce** (with Separation Agreement as exhibit)
- [ ] **Verification of Residency** (60 days in county)
- [ ] Financial Disclosure forms (if required by your county)
- [ ] Proposed **Decree of Divorce / Judgment and Decree**
- [ ] If children: **Parenting Plan**; **Child Support Worksheet** per Wyoming Guidelines

---

## Phase 5 — File at Wyoming District Court

- [ ] File Complaint + Separation Agreement + all exhibits at District Court clerk's office
- [ ] Pay **$75–$100** filing fee (exact amount varies by county)
- [ ] Receive case number

---

## Phase 6 — Service and 20-Day Waiting Period

- [ ] If joint filing: both sign; service may not be required
- [ ] If individual filing: serve Defendant; Defendant has **20 days** to respond
- [ ] **Wyoming 20-day waiting period** — measured from filing or service; one of the shortest in the US

---

## Phase 7 — Final Hearing or Entry on Papers

- [ ] Many Wyoming District Courts hold a brief final hearing for uncontested divorces
- [ ] If hearing: confirm residency, grounds, agreement is voluntary, child support compliance
- [ ] Decree of Divorce entered

---

## Phase 8 — Post-Divorce Steps

- [ ] Real estate deed: Quitclaim Deed → **Wyoming County Clerk** in the county where property is located
- [ ] QDRO for employer retirement plans
- [ ] Wyoming Retirement System (WRS): retirement.wyo.gov — DRO procedures for state employees
- [ ] Vehicles: Wyoming DMV
- [ ] Name restoration: Wyoming DMV → Social Security → accounts
- [ ] Update all beneficiary designations immediately
