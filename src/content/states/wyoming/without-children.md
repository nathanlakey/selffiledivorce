# Wyoming Divorce Without Children (2026)

With no minor children, Wyoming divorce is one of the simplest and most affordable in the country — $75–$100 fee, 20-day waiting period, and complete paperwork ready before filing.

---

## Overview

| Factor | Rule |
|---|---|
| Official term | Divorce |
| Court | District Court — county |
| Filing fee | $75–$100 (varies by county) |
| Residency | 60 days in the filing county |
| Waiting period | 20 days (one of shortest in US) |
| Separation Agreement | Filed with the Complaint |
| Property system | Equitable distribution |
| Alimony | Court discretion; **rarely awarded long-term** |

## Step-by-Step (No Children)

### 1 — Confirm 60-Day County Residency
Either spouse must have lived in Wyoming — in the filing county — for at least 60 days.

### 2 — Complete the Separation Agreement Before Filing
Wyoming practice: resolve all property, debts, and alimony before filing. File the Separation Agreement with the Complaint.

### 3 — File Complaint + Separation Agreement at Wyoming District Court
Pay $75–$100.

### 4 — Service (or Joint Filing)
If joint, service may not be required. If individual, serve Defendant (20-day response window).

### 5 — 20-Day Waiting Period
One of the shortest waiting periods in the United States.

### 6 — Final Hearing (Brief)
Most Wyoming District Courts schedule a brief hearing even for uncontested cases.

## Separation Agreement — What to Cover (No Children)

### Marital Real Property
Address, legal description, FMV, mortgage, equity, assignment.
- One keeps: buyout + refinancing deadline + Deed → **Wyoming County Clerk**
- Or sale: net proceeds split

### Marital Financial Accounts
Institution, type, balance; assignment; transfer

### Retirement Accounts
- QDRO for ERISA employer plans
- IRA: transfer incident to divorce
- Wyoming Retirement System (WRS): retirement.wyo.gov — DRO for state/public employees

### Vehicles
Assignment; loan assumption; Wyoming DMV

### Marital Debts
Creditor, balance, who assumes, indemnification

### Alimony
Wyoming courts rarely award long-term alimony. Most couples include an explicit waiver:
"Each party waives any and all claims for alimony, now and forever."

### Separate Property Acknowledgment
Confirm each spouse's separate property explicitly

## Post-Divorce Steps (No Children)

- Record Deed at **Wyoming County Clerk** in the county where property is located
- QDRO for employer retirement plans
- Wyoming WRS DRO: retirement.wyo.gov
- Wyoming DMV for vehicle titles
- Restore name: Wyoming DMV → Social Security → bank accounts
- Update all beneficiary designations immediately
