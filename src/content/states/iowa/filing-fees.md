# Iowa Dissolution of Marriage Filing Fees and Costs (2026)

Iowa's filing fee varies by county, typically ranging from **$185 to $220**. The Electronic Divorce path uses the same fee structure.

---

## Court Filing Fees

| Fee | Amount |
|---|---|
| Petition for Dissolution of Marriage | **$185–$220** (varies by Iowa county) |
| Counterclaim (Respondent) | $100–$150 (varies) |
| Certified copies of Decree | $10–$20 per copy |
| Filing fee waiver | Application required |

**Common Iowa county fees:**
- Polk County (Des Moines): ~$220
- Linn County (Cedar Rapids): ~$200
- Johnson County (Iowa City): ~$185
- Check your specific District Court's fee schedule before filing

---

## Fee Waiver — Application for Waiver of Filing Fees

Iowa waives filing fees for qualifying low-income filers.

**How to apply:**
1. Complete the **Application for Waiver of Fees** (IA forms at iowacourts.gov)
2. File with the Petition
3. Court reviews income and expenses
4. If approved: filing fee waived

**Eligibility:** Generally income at or below 125% of federal poverty level; receipt of public assistance; gross income insufficient to cover basic necessities.

---

## Electronic Divorce — Fee

The Electronic Divorce uses the same fee structure as the standard petition. There is no additional fee for using the online Electronic Divorce system.
