# West Virginia Divorce Filing Fees and Costs (2026)

---

## Court Filing Fee

| Fee | Amount |
|---|---|
| Petition for Divorce (Family Court) | **$135** |
| Certified copies of Final Order | $1–$5 per page |

---

## Service Costs (if one party files)

| Method | Estimated Cost |
|---|---|
| Sheriff's service | $30–$60 |
| Process server | $50–$100 |
| Waiver/Acceptance of Service (spouse signs) | Free |

For agreed (irreconcilable differences) divorces where the Respondent cooperates, a waiver of service eliminates service cost.

---

## Fee Waiver — Indigent Litigants

West Virginia waives filing fees for qualifying low-income filers.

**How to apply:**
1. Complete the **In Forma Pauperis** application (available at courtswv.gov)
2. File with the Petition
3. Judge approves; filing fee waived

**West Virginia Free Legal Answers:** lawv.net (Legal Aid of WV) — free legal help for qualifying individuals.
