# Delaware Divorce Filing Fees and Costs (2026)

---

## Court Filing Fee

| Fee | Amount |
|---|---|
| Petition for Divorce | **$165** |
| Certified copies of Final Decree | $1–$5 per page |

---

## Service Costs

| Method | Estimated Cost |
|---|---|
| Acceptance of Service (spouse signs) | Free |
| Sheriff's service | $30–$60 |
| Process server | $50–$125 |

---

## Fee Waiver — Financial Hardship

Delaware waives filing fees for qualifying low-income filers.

**How to apply:**
1. Complete the **Application to Proceed In Forma Pauperis** (Family Court form)
2. File with the Petition
3. Judge approves; fee waived

Forms: courts.delaware.gov/selfhelp

**Delaware Legal Help:** delawarelegalhelp.org — free legal assistance for qualifying individuals.
**Community Legal Aid Society:** clasi.org — free civil legal services.

---

## Recorder of Deeds — Deed Recording

After divorce, real estate deed changes are recorded at the **Delaware Recorder of Deeds** in the county where the property is located:
- New Castle County Recorder of Deeds
- Kent County Recorder of Deeds
- Sussex County Recorder of Deeds

Fee: approximately $30–$50 per document. Realty Transfer Tax may apply (divorce transfers may be exempt — confirm with the Recorder's office).
