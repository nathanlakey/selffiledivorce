# Delaware Divorce Checklist — Step-by-Step (2026)

---

## Phase 1 — Pre-Filing Assessment

- [ ] Confirm 6-month Delaware residency (at least one spouse)
- [ ] Confirm grounds: irreconcilable differences (6+ months) OR 6 months separation
- [ ] Gather all financial documents: income, bank accounts, investments, real estate, retirement, debts
- [ ] Identify Family Court location: New Castle (Wilmington) | Kent (Dover) | Sussex (Georgetown)
- [ ] Download forms at courts.delaware.gov/selfhelp

---

## Phase 2 — Financial Inventory

- [ ] All real estate: fair market value, mortgage balance, marital vs. separate
- [ ] Bank and investment accounts: balances, marital vs. separate
- [ ] Retirement accounts: total balance; marital portion (contributions during marriage)
- [ ] Vehicles: values and loans
- [ ] All debts: creditor, balance, marital vs. separate
- [ ] Business interests (if any)
- [ ] Monthly income and expense documentation (for Financial Report)

---

## Phase 3 — Complete Financial Reports (Required)

- [ ] Both parties complete their own **Financial Report** (Delaware Family Court form)
- [ ] Discloses monthly income, living expenses, all assets, all debts
- [ ] Both file with Family Court — **mandatory in all Delaware divorces**

---

## Phase 4 — Draft the Separation Agreement

- [ ] All marital real property addressed (assignment, buyout, sale; Quitclaim Deed → Delaware Recorder of Deeds)
- [ ] All marital financial accounts assigned
- [ ] Retirement accounts: QDRO for employer plans; IRA transfer language
- [ ] Vehicles assigned; Delaware DMV title transfer
- [ ] All marital debts assigned; indemnification language
- [ ] Separate property acknowledged and confirmed
- [ ] **Alimony:** award with terms — or explicit waiver
- [ ] If children: legal custody, physical custody, Parenting Plan, child support per Delaware Formula
- [ ] Both spouses sign and notarize the Separation Agreement

---

## Phase 5 — Complete Court Forms

Forms: courts.delaware.gov/selfhelp

- [ ] **Petition for Divorce** (Family Court form)
- [ ] **Summons**
- [ ] **Financial Report** (both parties)
- [ ] Acceptance of Service (if spouse cooperates)
- [ ] **Separation Agreement / Property Settlement Agreement**
- [ ] Proposed **Final Decree of Divorce**
- [ ] If children: **Parenting Plan**; **Child Support Worksheet** per Delaware Formula

---

## Phase 6 — File at Delaware Family Court

- [ ] File Petition and Financial Reports at Delaware **Family Court** in your county
- [ ] Pay **$165** filing fee
- [ ] Receive case number
- [ ] Serve Respondent (or obtain Acceptance of Service)
- [ ] File Return of Service / Acceptance of Service

---

## Phase 7 — No Waiting Period

- [ ] No mandatory waiting period in Delaware — proceed immediately
- [ ] Schedule hearing with Family Court calendar clerk

---

## Phase 8 — Final Hearing

- [ ] Appear at hearing
- [ ] Judge reviews Financial Reports, Separation Agreement, Parenting Plan (if applicable)
- [ ] Judge enters **Final Decree of Divorce**
- [ ] Obtain certified copies

---

## Phase 9 — Post-Divorce Steps

- [ ] Real estate deed: Quitclaim Deed → **Delaware Recorder of Deeds** in county where property is located
- [ ] QDRO for employer retirement plans
- [ ] Delaware state employees: State of Delaware Pension (stateemployees.delaware.gov) — DRO
- [ ] Vehicles: Delaware DMV
- [ ] Name restoration: Delaware DMV → Social Security → accounts
- [ ] Beneficiary designations: life insurance, retirement, payable-on-death accounts
