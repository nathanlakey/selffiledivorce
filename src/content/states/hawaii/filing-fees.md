## Fee Waiver — Financial Hardship

Hawaii waives filing fees for qualifying low-income filers.

**How to apply:**
1. Complete the **Affidavit for Waiver of Fees** (Form 1F-P-881)
2. File with the Complaint
3. Judge approves; fee waived

Forms: courts.state.hi.us/self-help

**Legal Aid Society of Hawaii:** legalaidhawaii.org — free legal help for qualifying individuals.

---

## Bureau of Conveyances — Deed Recording

Real estate deed changes are recorded at the **Hawaii Bureau of Conveyances** — a state-level office. Hawaii does not use county recorders for this purpose.

Bureau of Conveyances fee: approximately $25–$35 per instrument (for regular recording).
