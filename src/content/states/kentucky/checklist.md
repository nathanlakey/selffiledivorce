# Kentucky Dissolution of Marriage Checklist — Step-by-Step (2026)

---

## Phase 1 — Pre-Filing Preparation

- [ ] Confirm residency: either party has lived in Kentucky for 180+ days
- [ ] Identify filing county (Circuit Court in county where either spouse has lived 180+ days)
- [ ] Note date of separation — the 60-day wait starts at filing, but know your separation date
- [ ] Download AOC forms from courts.ky.gov/forms:
  - AOC-238.1 — Petition for Dissolution of Marriage
  - AOC-239.1 — Domestic Relations Financial Disclosure (both parties)
  - AOC-238.3 — Acceptance of Service / Waiver of Summons (if agreed)
  - Parenting Plan forms if minor children involved

---

## Phase 2 — Financial Inventory

Both spouses must complete the Domestic Relations Financial Disclosure (AOC-239.1). Gather:
- [ ] Last 2–3 pay stubs (both spouses)
- [ ] Last 2–3 years federal and Kentucky state tax returns
- [ ] All bank and investment account statements
- [ ] Retirement account statements (note pre-marital balance)
- [ ] Mortgage statement and property value estimate
- [ ] Vehicle values (KBB or NADA) and loan balances
- [ ] All credit card and debt statements
- [ ] Business interest documentation if applicable

---

## Phase 3 — Complete AOC Forms

- [ ] Petition for Dissolution of Marriage (AOC-238.1) — complete all sections
- [ ] Domestic Relations Financial Disclosure (AOC-239.1) — Petitioner's
- [ ] Draft Separation Agreement covering all marital property and debts
- [ ] If children: Parenting Plan, Child Support Worksheet (AOC-MISC.3)

---

## Phase 4 — File at Circuit Court

- [ ] Take original + 2 copies to the Circuit Court Clerk in filing county
- [ ] Pay $113–$148 filing fee (or file IFP motion if qualifying)
- [ ] Clerk assigns case number and issues Summons

---

## Phase 5 — Serve the Respondent

- [ ] **Easiest (agreed cases):** Respondent signs Acceptance of Service / Waiver of Summons (AOC-238.3)
- [ ] **Certified mail:** Return receipt card serves as proof of service
- [ ] **Sheriff/process server:** Personal service; return of service filed with court
- [ ] File proof of service with Circuit Court Clerk

---

## Phase 6 — 60-Day Waiting Period

- [ ] Mark Day 61 on calendar — earliest date decree can be entered
- [ ] Respondent files Response or Acceptance within 20/30 days
- [ ] Finalize and execute Separation Agreement (both parties sign, notarize)
- [ ] Respondent completes their Financial Disclosure (AOC-239.1)

---

## Phase 7 — Submit Final Documents

- [ ] File fully executed Separation Agreement with court
- [ ] File proposed Decree of Dissolution of Marriage
- [ ] File both parties' Financial Disclosures
- [ ] If children: file executed Parenting Plan and Child Support Worksheet
- [ ] Most uncontested cases: no hearing required — judge reviews paperwork

---

## Phase 8 — Post-Decree

- [ ] Obtain certified copies of Decree from Circuit Court Clerk ($5–$15 per copy)
- [ ] Real estate: execute deed, record at County Clerk's office
- [ ] QDRO: initiate for any employer retirement plans
- [ ] IRA transfers
- [ ] Update beneficiary designations
- [ ] Name restoration: Social Security Administration → Kentucky Transportation Cabinet → financial accounts
