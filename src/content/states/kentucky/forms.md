# Kentucky Dissolution of Marriage Forms — AOC Forms Guide (2026)

Kentucky's Administrative Office of Courts (AOC) publishes numbered forms for every step of the dissolution process. All forms are **free** at **courts.ky.gov/forms**.

---

## Where to Get Kentucky Dissolution Forms

**Official source:** courts.ky.gov/forms

Search for "dissolution" or "domestic relations" to find the full AOC form set. Forms are PDF — fill in on your computer or by hand. Most Kentucky Circuit Courts require paper filing for self-represented parties.

---

## Core Forms — Uncontested Dissolution

| AOC Form | Title | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| AOC-238.1 | Petition for Dissolution of Marriage | Primary initiating document |
| AOC-238.2 | Summons | Court-issued; served with Petition |
| AOC-238.3 | Acceptance of Service / Waiver of Summons | Respondent signs to waive formal service |
| AOC-238.4 | Response to Petition for Dissolution | Respondent's answer (contested) |
| AOC-239.1 | Domestic Relations Financial Disclosure | Required from BOTH parties |
| AOC-238.7 | Decree of Dissolution of Marriage | Final order — signed by judge |

**Note:** AOC form numbers may be updated. Always download the current version from courts.ky.gov.

---

## Domestic Relations Financial Disclosure (AOC-239.1)

Required from **both** spouses in every dissolution case. Discloses:
- Monthly income from all sources
- Monthly expenses
- All assets (real property, vehicles, financial accounts, retirement, personal property)
- All liabilities (mortgages, loans, credit cards)

Both spouses sign the Financial Disclosure under penalty of perjury. File with the court.

---

## Separation Agreement

Kentucky requires that property, debt, and support issues be resolved by a **Separation Agreement** in uncontested cases. The Separation Agreement is filed with the court and incorporated into the Decree.

**The Separation Agreement must address:**
- All marital property: real estate, vehicles, financial accounts, retirement, personal property
- All marital debts: assignment to each spouse; indemnification
- Maintenance (alimony): award, waiver, or reservation
- If children: legal custody, physical custody, visitation, child support (must comply with Guidelines)
- Name restoration if requested

**Format:** No official AOC form for the Separation Agreement — parties draft their own or use legal aid templates. File with the Circuit Court.

## Forms With Children

| Form | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Parenting Plan | Custody and visitation schedule — required when minor children involved |
| AOC-MISC.3 (or local equivalent) | Child Support Worksheet — calculates support per Kentucky Guidelines |
| Income Withholding Order (AOC form) | Wage withholding for child support — required unless both parties agree otherwise |

**Child custody terminology in Kentucky:**
- "Sole custody" vs. "Joint custody"
- "Primary residential parent" — parent with whom child primarily lives
- "Timesharing schedule" or "parenting schedule" — the visitation arrangement

---

## Kentucky Child Support Guidelines

Kentucky uses a guideline-based system. The support amount is determined by:
- Both parents' combined monthly gross income
- Number of children
- Child care costs
- Health insurance premiums
- Parenting time adjustment

Complete the Child Support Worksheet. Kentucky's guidelines are in KRS Chapter 403.
