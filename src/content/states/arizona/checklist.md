# Arizona Dissolution Checklist — Step-by-Step (2026)

Use this checklist to track every stage. Arizona's 60-day waiting period starts from service — work to serve early.

---

## Phase 1 — Pre-Filing Preparation

- [ ] Confirm 90-day residency requirement is met
- [ ] Confirm this is NOT a covenant marriage
- [ ] Gather all financial documents:
  - Bank and investment account statements
  - Retirement account statements
  - Mortgage statement (or lease if renting)
  - Vehicle loan statements
  - Credit card statements
  - Pay stubs or other income documentation
  - Last 2 years of tax returns
- [ ] List all community property (acquired during marriage)
- [ ] List all separate property (pre-marital, gifts, inheritances)
- [ ] List all community debts

---

## Phase 2 — Generate Forms

- [ ] Go to **selfservecenter.azcourts.gov**
- [ ] Complete the guided interview (Dissolution with or without children)
- [ ] Download and print the generated form packet
- [ ] Review each form for accuracy
- [ ] Sign and notarize where required

---

## Phase 3 — File at Superior Court

- [ ] Identify the correct Superior Court (county where you reside)
- [ ] Make 3 copies of the complete packet
- [ ] File at the Clerk of Court
- [ ] Pay filing fee ($338–$364)
- [ ] Receive case number and filed-stamped copies

---

## Phase 4 — Serve the Respondent

- [ ] Choose service method (Acceptance of Service, process server, sheriff, mail)
- [ ] Complete service
- [ ] Note the service date — **60-day clock starts here**
- [ ] File Proof of Service (or Acceptance of Service) with the court

---

## Phase 5 — 60-Day Waiting Period

- [ ] Track the response deadline (20 days in-state, 30 days out-of-state)
- [ ] Negotiate final terms of Consent Decree during this period
- [ ] If children: complete Parenting Plan and Child Support calculation
- [ ] Prepare Consent Decree of Dissolution (use Self-Service Center)
- [ ] Both spouses review and sign

---

## Phase 6 — File the Consent Decree (Agreed Case)

- [ ] Confirm 60 days have passed from service date
- [ ] File the complete packet:
  - Consent Decree of Dissolution
  - Affidavit of Compliance (confirming 60-day period passed)
  - If children: Parenting Plan, Child Support Order, Parent's Worksheet
- [ ] Court reviews and judge signs (no hearing for agreed cases)

---

## Phase 7 — Post-Dissolution

- [ ] Request 3–5 **certified copies** of the Decree of Dissolution
- [ ] **Name restoration:** SSA → Arizona MVD → bank accounts
- [ ] **Real estate deed:** Record new deed at county Recorder's office
- [ ] **Vehicle titles:** Arizona MVD
- [ ] **Retirement accounts:** QDRO for employer plans; IRA transfer for IRAs
- [ ] **Beneficiary designations:** Update all accounts immediately
- [ ] **Health insurance:** Arrange new coverage within 60 days of dissolution
