# Nevada Divorce Without Children (2026)

With no minor children, Nevada divorce is the fastest in the country. No waiting period. 6-week residency. Joint Petition means no service of process. The focus is on the Marital Settlement Agreement covering community property, debts, and spousal support.

## Joint Petition — Fastest Path

**Both spouses file together.** No one is the Petitioner or Respondent. No service of process. No response deadline. Both parties are equally represented in the filing.

**Steps:**
1. Both parties sign the **Joint Petition for Divorce (No Children)**
2. Both sign the **Marital Settlement Agreement** (if property/debts exist)
3. Both provide a **Resident Witness Affidavit** (or one for the filing party)
4. File at District Court; pay $284–$320
5. Judge reviews; enters **Decree of Divorce** when satisfied

## Marital Settlement Agreement — What to Include (No Children)

All community property must be addressed. The MSA can deviate from 50/50 by agreement.

### Community Real Property
- Full legal description; agreed value; mortgage balance; net equity
- Assignment: one spouse keeps (buyout; refinancing deadline; Grant Deed → County Recorder) or sell (proceeds split; listing terms)

### Community Financial Accounts
- Each account: institution, type, balance; assignment; transfer instructions

### Community Retirement Accounts
- QDRO for employer plans (marital/community portion from marriage date to separation date)
- IRA: transfer incident to divorce
- Nevada PERS: contact PERS directly for domestic relations order requirements

### Community Vehicles
- Assign each vehicle; assume the loan; Nevada DMV title transfer

### Community Debts
- All joint debts: creditor, balance, who assumes, indemnification

### Spousal Support
Either award with specific terms — or explicit waiver:
"Each party waives any and all claims for spousal support, now and forever. This waiver is final and non-modifiable."

### Separate Property
Acknowledge any significant separate property to prevent future disputes.
